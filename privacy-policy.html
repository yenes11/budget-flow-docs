Introduction

Welcome to Budget Flow ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our approach to data and privacy when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any data.

Budget Flow is designed with complete privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or have access to:

  • Personal information

Local Data Storage

All the data you enter into Budget Flow (such as transactions, budgets, and categories) is stored exclusively on your device. This data:

  • Is not transmitted to any server

Third-Party Services

Budget Flow does not integrate with any third-party analytics, tracking, or data collection services. Your privacy is fully protected.

Your Data, Your Control

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Access: You have full access to all your data within the app
  • Deletion: You can delete all your data by uninstalling the app or clearing app data in your device settings
  • Portability: Your data stays with your device

Children's Privacy

Since we do not collect any data from any users, we also do not collect any data from children. Budget Flow can be safely used by users of all ages.
